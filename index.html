ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Precious Candy Abillonar Prado. I'm a Senior High School Grade 11 student with a passion for exploring languages and connecting with people from around the world. Ever since I was very little, I've been fascinated with learning languages and reading.
At 12, I discovered Arduino, which opened my eyes to computer programming and electronics—sparking a lifelong love for mathematics, my favorite subject. I also enjoy creative hobbies like playing with dolls and sewing adorable coquette dresses for them.
I'm inspired by authors such as Mary Shelley and Taylor Caldwell, and I love listening to Russian and French songs. My interests extend into the world of horror and gothic aesthetics, and I find joy in playing Sims 4, playing the ukulele and flute,
skating, painting with gouache in my little sketchbook, practicing calligraphy, and even experimenting with cyberpunk aesthetics.
Writing is a constant in my life. I not only keep a diary to capture my thoughts and dreams, but I also write about my nightmares, transforming them into stories that help me express my deepest emotions.
I invite you to explore my portfolio gallery and join me on this exciting journey of learning, creativity, and self-expression.
